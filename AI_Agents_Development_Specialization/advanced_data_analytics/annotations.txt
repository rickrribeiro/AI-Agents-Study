AI Planning and Problem Breakdown

Break down large tasks into smaller, manageable steps to avoid overwhelming the AI tool and to work within output limits.
Create separate plans for different goals (e.g., blog post vs. social media posts) to handle each part individually.
Refining and Editing Plans

Start with a generic plan and then refine it based on the actual content to make it more specific and actionable.
Edit the plan by removing unnecessary steps before execution to streamline the process.
Executing Plans Step-by-Step

Execute each step individually, saving outputs in separate files to track progress and avoid losing work.
At the start of each step, review the previous step’s output to ensure alignment and cohesion.
Act as a conductor by guiding the AI to continue or adjust when it hits output limits or misses details.
Finally, combine all individual pieces into a single cohesive document or file for the final product.
